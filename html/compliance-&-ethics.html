Social Compliance

Compliance Vision

To preserve the standing of our clients, business associates, and company by making sure that our audit procedures are conducted with unwavering ethics, honesty, and technological know-how. Our compliance team initially conducts a social compliance check at the plant before placing an order if the audit is successful. We abide with local legislation and the Buyers Code of Conduct in this regard. We routinely keep an eye on all of our contracted vendors' and suppliers' compliance concerns, both known and unknown.

CSR (Corporate Social Responsibility)

CSR

Sewing Tex Apparels exclusively chooses and collaborates with manufacturers who take social responsibility seriously and implement practical methods to improve the surrounding environment and community. This not only strengthens our morals but also provides our clients with the impression that their business with us results in something more positive than just financial dealings. Our code of conduct requires that all of our corporate partners be concerned about the ecosystem and the welfare of their employees.
