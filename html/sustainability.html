Sustainability

Elegant in every stitches

Sewing Tex Apparels Limited have a policy emphasizing sustainable practices in fabric production, energy-efficient manufacturing processes, and eco-friendly supply chain management.

Environmental Policy

We engage in projects such as recycling old garments, adopting green production techniques, or supporting community programs for environmental awareness.

Green Initiatives

We focus on using organic cotton, recycled polyester, or other sustainable textiles in their clothing production.

Sustainable Materials

The company employ energy-efficient sewing machines, use LED lighting in factories, or power operations with renewable energy like solar or wind.

Energy Efficiency

As garment production involves significant water usage, Sewing Tex Apparels could adopt water-saving dyeing processes, treat and reuse wastewater, or use waterless technologies.

Water Conservation

Strategies might include reducing fabric waste through precise cutting techniques, recycling fabric scraps, or implementing take-back programs for old clothes.
