05

Production

Experienced & Dedicated Production Team

Sewing Tex has produced a variety of RMG goods in over 14 factories, all of which are comply plants. There is virtually no possibility of orders overlapping because we have several distinct factories for our knit, woven, denim, and sweater manufacturing. These factories exclusively hire seasoned personnel for optimal productivity, and they are always cautious and attentive to worker welfare. Given Sewing Tex's longstanding commitment to women's empowerment, we are pleased to report that over 70% of all employees across all of our factories are female.

  • The consumer first approves the PP sample.
  • Designs are complete.
  • The manufacturing procedure has been set out in its entirety.
  • Fabric of choice is produced or sourced.
06

QA and QC

Experienced quality assurance team

A group of skilled and knowledgeable QAs and QCs work for Sewing Tex. They work around the clock at all manufacturing units, participating in every step of the process as soon as pre-cutting permission is granted.

In order to ensure that everything is up to par, the QCs assigned to our factories do size, set, inline, midline, pre-final, and final inspections in addition to continuously monitoring production.

The Quality & Merchant team reviews and signs all inspection reports.

Only if the outcomes of our pre-final examination are satisfied may the final inspection be completed. The items are prepared for shipment following final inspection and customer acceptance of all quality.

07

Delivery and Shipment

Delivery and Shipment

Both clothing manufacturers and importers place a high value on timely delivery and proper packaging. Proper and accurate packaging and secure delivery assist prevent damage to the items and maintain their proper appearance when ironed.

Our commercial and shipment team works diligently to expedite the entire procedure and guarantee a seamless delivery in the products delivery and shipment department. In essence, we work on FOB and guarantee that the items are delivered to the forwarder designated by the buyer. Additionally, based on requirements and quick delivery, our shipping department assists all clients in choosing the finest shipping and forwarding business.

Knitting

Advanced knitting techniques

The most up-to-date equipment is available in our knitting factories, which include machines for nearly all of the major and frequently used gauges in both tube and open diameter. In order to produce more quickly, our machines may operate at high RPMs.
