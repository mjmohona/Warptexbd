About Sewing Tex Apparels

The Top Producer and Exporter of Clothing

Sewing Tex Apparels Limited is a well-known international clothing sourcing firm, manufacturer, and dedicated exporter of knit, sweaters, denim, woven, and other items. Two partners worked together to incorporate the business in 2010. For all of our globally recognized brands, importers, and department stores from the US, RU, and EU markets, we are dedicated to producing the highest quality clothing. Since our founding 13 years ago, we have established enduring business partnerships with the majority of our global clients and gained their confidence in their corresponding marketplaces. All types of RMG products, including men's, women's, boys', girls', and children's, as well as unisex goods such pants, shirts, dresses, sweaters, socks, pullovers, cardigans, jumpers, vests, t-shirts, polo shirts, sweatshirts, jogging suits, etc., are our specialty.

Our strong group of qualified professionals is made up of vibrant, seasoned people. From product creation until the final dispatch, our unbeatable lead guarantees flawless service. Following all local and international regulations that are relevant to our field of activity has always been our serious commitment. Our sourcing team is made up of highly skilled sourcing experts with in-depth industry expertise to guarantee the highest quality items. Our seasoned quality control professionals have years of expertise and ensure that the client receives precisely what they requested. To ensure a smooth sailing to the customer's preferred port, we collaborate with the top C&F agencies in the nation for the shipment operations.Since the outset, we have made a name for ourselves as a one-stop shop for all of our global clients' product sourcing, design, production, quality control, and sustainable supply chain needs.

We have been monitoring all compliance concerns and international certifications for Sustainable Development & Productions since the beginning since the majority of well-known brands deal with Sewingtex. Every one of our factories and production locations complies completely. Additionally, we employ certified sustainable washing facilities (DETOX, BEPI, BSCI, GOTS, etc.). We are excited to collaborate with manufacturers who adhere to this code and prioritize worker safety in light of the "Green factory" concept. We envision a society in which all stakeholders are content and receive the results they are entitled to, allowing hard labor and peace of mind to coexist harmoniously.
